ABOUT

WHAT DO WE DO?

The service we provide will help you say goodbye to your family pet with love and affection they deserve. Our pet cemetery is specifically designed for pets and parents who genuinely love and cherish the bonds they have.

As you enter our cemetery's gates, you will witness our lovely gardens with walks and benches, and a serene setting for remembering happy, loving moments. Funerals are not simply for humans. Our pet funeral services enable you to arrange and hold a memorial for your pet, which helps you achieve these goals by providing closure and allowing you to cherish the time you had together.

SERVICES

Grief Support
Grief Support is included in every service we provide. Grief Support is a grief guide that offers additional support networks and contacts to assist you get through this trying period. It also includes advice on how to grieve.

We've all lost cherished pets, so we understand how important it is to have the appropriate resources and a network of people to lean on. We are here for you if you need a listening ear, a place to share your sorrow with others, or just someone to chat to.
Burial Services
You can choose from a variety of caskets, vaults, urns, and memorial markers at our funeral homes' fully equipped showrooms. Pet parents who want to spend a few final moments with their cherished family member may do so in a serene and peaceful chapel. The cost of burial services varies according to the type, size, and accommodation required.

The ability to select your pet's burial location from one of our lovely gardens, your pet's quality coffin, and your pet's personalized headstone all provide a peace that only you can understand. Furthermore, this package also includes a wide variety of flowers, for you to choose from, to be planted on their grave.
Cremation Services
The option to keep your pet's cremated remains as a long-lasting keepsake is offered by pet cremation services. When you select cremation, your beloved pet is placed in the crematory and walls are used to keep them apart from the other animals. We make sure that you only receive your pet's cremated ashes when the cremation process is finished.

Your pet's distinctive barcode travels with them during the transportation and cremation processes thanks to our proprietary personalized pet tracking technology, ensuring that you receive your pet's cremains. Furthermore, these cremated remains can be solidified and turn into stones, placed in jewelry, buried in a cemetery, or kept in your home in an urn which is also a service we offer.

VALUE PROPOSITION

Pawradise understands the significance of each visit to your loved ones. We want to maximize the experience by making sure every minute you spend at the park is worth your while. Memorial parks serve the transcendental purpose of providing a final resting place where family members can mourn and honor the dead for many years. We ensure that our parks have everything families will need, more so during times they need to gather in good spirits. These facilities aim to better support those grieving. Memorial parks also evolved. While the solemn ambiance is preserved, these places are for celebrating life as well. A vital part of the grieving process includes spending time at the park and paying tribute to how the pet lived their life. These fun activities will help sustain the feeling of connectedness with the dearly departed and those left behind.
